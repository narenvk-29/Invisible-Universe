# The Invisible Universe - Poster Research Guide

## Overview

Your poster explores the **"Invisible Universe"** - the hidden components and phenomena that make up most of our cosmos but cannot be directly seen with our eyes or traditional telescopes.

---

## Two Main Topics

### 1. **Dark Matter** (via Bullet Cluster)
- **What's Invisible**: Dark matter itself - only detectable through gravity
- **Evidence**: Bullet Cluster collision showing separation of visible and invisible matter
- **Percentage**: ~27% of universe is dark matter

### 2. **Big Bang & Cosmic Evolution**
- **What's Invisible**: Early universe conditions, cosmic microwave background, dark energy
- **Evidence**: CMB radiation, universe expansion, element abundances
- **Percentage**: ~68% dark energy, ~5% normal matter

**Together**: 95% of the universe is "invisible" (dark matter + dark energy)!

## The "Invisible Universe" Theme

### What We Cannot See Directly
1. **Dark Matter** - Invisible matter holding galaxies together
2. **Dark Energy** - Mysterious force accelerating expansion
3. **Cosmic Microwave Background** - Invisible to eyes (microwave radiation)
4. **Early Universe** - Events billions of years ago
5. **Gravitational Effects** - Gravity itself is invisible

### How We Detect the Invisible
1. **Gravitational Lensing** - Light bending reveals dark matter
2. **X-ray Telescopes** - See hot gas invisible to optical telescopes
3. **Microwave Detectors** - Reveal CMB radiation
4. **Redshift Measurements** - Detect universe expansion
5. **Mathematical Models** - Predict what we cannot observe
