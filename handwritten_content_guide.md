## General Handwriting Tips:

### Make It Look Natural:
- Use real handwriting (neat but not perfect)
- Vary letter sizes slightly
- Cross out and correct one or two words
- Add small doodles and sketches
- Use different colored highlighters
- Underline key phrases
- Add exclamation marks for emphasis!!!
- Circle important numbers/facts

### Visual Elements:
- Draw stars â­ and sparkles âœ¨
- Add arrows â†’ connecting ideas
- Sketch simple diagrams
- Use boxes for key facts
- Border decorations
- Question marks â“ for mysteries
- Checkmarks âœ“ for proven facts

### Color Coding:
- **Titles**: Bold marker (contrasting color)
- **Main text**: Black pen
- **Highlights**: Yellow, green, blue, orange
- **Emphasis**: Red or purple marker
- **Decorations**: Any bright colors

### Paper Orientation:
- All can be portrait (vertical)
- Or mix portrait and landscape for variety
- Leave margins for visual breathing room
